## 📌 Project Overview
This project focuses on developing essential digital literacy skills required in today’s digital world. It includes practical tasks related to online safety, professional communication, digital tools, and cyber awareness.

The project was completed as part of the Digital Literacy course, where I performed the role of a Student Digital Ambassador to promote responsible and effective use of digital platforms among students.

---

## 🎯 Objectives
- To understand the concept of digital literacy  
- To build a professional digital portfolio  
- To learn safe and responsible internet practices  
- To improve communication skills through professional emails  
- To gain awareness about cybercrime and prevention  

---

## 📂 Tasks Completed

 🔹 Task 1: Digital Literacy Infographic
Created an infographic using Canva covering topics like digital literacy, safe internet practices, and useful digital tools.

 🔹 Task 2: Digital Portfolio
Developed profiles on platforms such as GitHub, LinkedIn, and HackerRank to build a professional online presence.

🔹 Task 3: Coding & Collaboration
Solved a beginner-level coding problem on HackerRank and created a Google Form quiz for digital literacy awareness.

🔹 Task 4: Professional Communication
Drafted two professional emails and created a social media Do’s and Don’ts checklist.

🔹 Task 5: Cybercrime Awareness
Prepared a case study on phishing and created a prevention checklist to promote safe online practices.
