Stay Safe Online – Prevention Checklist

- Do not share OTP, passwords, or bank details with anyone  
- Always verify website URLs before entering information  
- Avoid clicking on suspicious links or unknown emails  
- Use strong and unique passwords  
- Enable two-factor authentication (2FA)  
- Use official apps and websites for payments  
- Avoid using public Wi-Fi for financial transactions  
- Install antivirus software and keep it updated  

In case of cyber fraud, report immediately at:
National Cyber Crime Portal: https://www.cybercrime.gov.in  
Helpline Number: 1930
